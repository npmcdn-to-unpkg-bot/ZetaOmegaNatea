SONUS 4

From $2,300

Simply stated, a Jazz bass with attitude. Voiced for cut and clarity, the 519 with its’ three band, selectable mids, active system, gives you tones from Jaco to Marcus and everything in between. As a thirty-five inch scale bass, the low B-string has a slightly increased focus for a more piano like tone while the 19 mm string spacing allows for advanced right hand slap technique. In combination with the ash body, this bass has a voice with solid lows, punchy mids and a bright top end.

Recommended for: rock, jazz, funk and slap
